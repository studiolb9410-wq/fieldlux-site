Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Last updated · 2026-04-23

1. Who we are

FieldLux is a projection preflight tool operated from the Republic of Korea. This page describes what we collect when you request beta access via the signup form on the landing page. For questions about this policy, email jay@field-lux.com.

2. What we collect

When you submit the beta request form, we store the following:

  • Email address — required. Used to send your beta access link and related updates.
  • Role — required. The dropdown value you selected (Technical Director, Projection Designer, etc.). Helps us prioritise onboarding.
  • Use case — optional. The free-text line describing what you'd like to preflight.
  • Browser metadata — automatically captured: User-Agent string (truncated to 500 chars) and HTTP Referrer (truncated to 500 chars). Used for abuse detection and to understand which integration partners send the most relevant traffic.

We do not use cookies, trackers, analytics scripts, or fingerprinting on the landing page. We do not collect IP addresses beyond the short-term server log retained by our hosting provider (Vercel) and database provider (Supabase) for operational purposes.

3. How we use it

  • To review your beta request and send you an access invitation when a slot opens.
  • To send occasional beta-related updates (major releases, calibration changes, end-of-beta notice). Typically fewer than one message per month.
  • To understand the professional mix of our waitlist so we can prioritise features that match the real user base.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not operate a marketing mailing list.

4. Where it's stored

Waitlist records are stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database (fieldlux_waitlist table). Row-level security is configured so that the anonymous browser key used by this landing page can insert new records but cannot read, modify, or delete any record — your email is never readable from another browser session.

Supabase hosts our database in the region stated in our project settings. Data transferred to Supabase travels over TLS.

5. How long we keep it

  • Active beta waitlist: until you either accept an access invitation or request removal.
  • After beta ends: we will either migrate accepted users to a product account (with a new privacy notice) or delete unaccepted records within 90 days of beta closure.
  • On your request: we delete your record within 14 days of receiving a request at jay@field-lux.com.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • See what we have on file about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Have your record deleted.
  • Withdraw consent at any time — doing so removes you from the beta waitlist.
  • Object to the processing described above.

To exercise any of these, email jay@field-lux.com from the address you signed up with. We respond within 14 days.

7. Regional notes

Republic of Korea (PIPA): Collection and use of the items in §2 is based on your explicit consent given via the checkbox on the signup form. You may withdraw consent at any time using the address in §6; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

European Economic Area / UK (GDPR): The lawful basis for processing is consent under Article 6(1)(a). The Controller is FieldLux; contact details above. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

California (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we change anything. Material changes (new categories of data, new purposes, new processors) will be announced via the email address you signed up with before they take effect. Continued use of your beta access after a change means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

All privacy questions, requests, and complaints: jay@field-lux.com.
